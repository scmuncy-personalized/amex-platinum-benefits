## Credit details and gotchas

### Hotel Credit — $300 per half ($600/yr)
Prepaid Fine Hotels + Resorts (FHR) or The Hotel Collection (THC) bookings
through Amex Travel. THC requires 2+ night stays. FHR bookings also come
with daily breakfast for two, room upgrade when available, guaranteed 4pm
late checkout, and a ~$100 property credit — so a single FHR night often
returns $400+ in total value. **Gotcha:** must be *prepaid* through Amex
Travel; pay-at-hotel rates don't qualify.

### Resy Dining Credit — $100/quarter ($400/yr)
Eligible purchases at 10,000+ U.S. Resy-affiliated restaurants. No need to
book through Resy — just pay with the Platinum at a participating
restaurant. **Gotcha:** enrollment required; credit doesn't roll over
between quarters.

### Digital Entertainment — $25/month ($300/yr)
Covers Disney+ (and bundles), Hulu, ESPN+, Peacock, Paramount+, The New
York Times, The Wall Street Journal, YouTube Premium, YouTube TV. Applies
automatically to qualifying charges after enrollment. **Gotcha:** must be
billed directly by the provider (not through app stores).

### Equinox — $300/yr
Equinox club membership or Equinox+ digital subscription, auto-renewing,
charged to the card. Worth $0 if you wouldn't otherwise pay for Equinox.

### lululemon — $75/quarter ($300/yr)
U.S. lululemon stores or lululemon.com. **Gotcha:** excludes outlets; no
gift-card workaround.

### CLEAR Plus — ~$209/yr
Covers CLEAR Plus membership billed monthly to the card. Only valuable if
your airports have CLEAR lanes.

### Uber Cash — $15/month, $20 in December ($200/yr)
Auto-deposited to the linked Uber account monthly; works for rides and Uber
Eats (U.S.). **Gotcha:** does not roll over — unused monthly Uber Cash
vanishes.

### Oura Ring — $200 one-time per cardmember year
Hardware purchase at ouraring.com. **Gotcha:** hardware only, not the
subscription; resets on cardmember anniversary, not Jan 1.

### Airline Fee Credit — $200/yr
Incidental fees (checked bags, seat selection, in-flight purchases, lounge
day passes) on **one designated airline** selected in January and locked
for the year. **Gotcha:** airfare itself doesn't qualify; gift-card
workarounds are mostly dead.

### Walmart+ — $12.95/month (~$155/yr)
Covers monthly Walmart+ membership (includes free delivery, fuel discounts,
bundled Paramount+ or Peacock). **Gotcha:** monthly plan only; "Plus
Upgrades" not eligible.

### Uber One — $120/yr
Covers an auto-renewing Uber One membership charged to the card. Separate
from monthly Uber Cash.

### Global Entry / TSA PreCheck / NEXUS — up to $120
Application fee reimbursement every 4–4.5 years when paid with the card.

### Saks — ENDED
The $50 semiannual Saks credit ended July 1, 2026. Amex has said
replacement retail offers are coming; watch for announcements.

## Non-credit benefits

### Lounge access (Global Lounge Collection)
- **Centurion Lounges:** unlimited visits. Guests: 2 free with $75,000
  annual spend, otherwise $50/guest. Since July 8, 2026, guests must be on
  the same flight.
- **Priority Pass Select:** enrollment required.
- **Delta Sky Club:** 10 visits per Medallion Year (Feb 1–Jan 31) when
  flying Delta.
- Plus Escape Lounges, Plaza Premium, and partner lounges.

### Elite status (automatic or enrollment)
- **Hilton Honors Gold** and **Marriott Bonvoy Gold** — upgrades, bonus
  points, breakfast at many properties.
- **Car rental:** Hertz President's Circle, Avis Preferred Plus, National
  Emerald Club Executive (enrollment required).

### Travel programs
- **Fine Hotels + Resorts / The Hotel Collection** perks (see Hotel Credit).
- **International Airline Program:** discounted premium-cabin fares.
- **Cruise Privileges Program:** onboard credits on qualifying bookings.

### Protections
- Purchase Protection: up to $10,000/claim.
- Return Protection: up to $300/item.
- Extended Warranty: +1 year.
- Trip Delay Insurance: up to $500/trip after 6-hour delay.
- Trip Cancellation/Interruption: up to $10,000/trip.
- Cell Phone Protection: up to $800/claim, 2 claims/yr (pay the monthly
  phone bill with the card to activate).

## Earning rates
- 5x Membership Rewards on flights booked directly with airlines or via
  Amex Travel (up to $500k/calendar year).
- 5x on prepaid hotels via Amex Travel.
- 1x everything else.
